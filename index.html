What I can help your business with
01
Business Consulting

Aiding businesses in recognizing areas for improvement, outlining current processes, and suggesting process modifications and automation tools to simplify workflows, consequently refining their operations and enhancing overall efficiency.

02
IT Strategy and Planning

As an IT strategy consultant, I can help businesses develop and implement technology strategies that align with their overall business goals. This could include assessing existing systems, identifying areas for improvement, and creating a roadmap for future technology investments.

03
Data Analytics and Business Intelligence

Many companies are looking to gain insights from their data in order to make better decisions. As a data analytics consultant, I will guide businesses to develop data strategies, implement analytics tools, and create dashboards and reports to visualize and communicate insights.


04
Cybersecurity Consulting

With the increasing threat of cyberattacks, businesses are looking for consultants to help them identify and mitigate potential security risks. I can provide guidance to assess your company's security posture, recommend security controls, and provide guidance on incident response planning.

05
ERP and CRM Consulting

Many businesses rely on enterprise resource planning (ERP) and customer relationship management (CRM) systems to manage their operations and customer interactions. As an ERP or CRM consultant, I could help your business select, implement, and customize these systems to meet your specific needs.

06
Cloud Computing Consulting

As more businesses move their IT infrastructure to the cloud, there is a growing need for consultants to help them navigate this complex landscape. As a cloud computing consultant, I am able to assess your business' cloud readiness, select the right cloud platforms and services, and migrate your systems to the cloud.

About me
Skill through experience

I am a highly effective and reliable professional with practical experience in a diverse range of technologies and concepts. As a technical consultant for over a decade, my focus has been on business development and business intelligence, providing complex and robust solutions for real-world requirements. I have worked on long-term projects for enterprise clients, utilizing my expertise to deliver results that exceed expectations.

Since college, I have worked as an engineer for an Irish start-up that later became a Silicon Valley company. I was then headhunted by a consultancy firm in Prague, where I have provided consulting services for the past decade. Additionally, I have consulted with small businesses and start-ups on various technical aspects. Having worked as the IT director for the agency that hired me, I have gained experience with a wide range of technologies in the business domain, enabling me to provide high-quality consulting services to clients.

What I can do for you

As a technical consultant with extensive experience, I provide valuable guidance to small businesses, startups, and entrepreneurs. My expertise includes business development, process improvement, digital transformation, and other technical aspects. I help businesses identify areas for improvement, recommend solutions, and provide guidance on implementing and optimizing technology solutions to enhance overall efficiency.
